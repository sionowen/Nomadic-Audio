Nomadic Audio

Offering Professional Location Recording & Project Mixing Services

What We Do

Nomadic Audio specializes in location-based recording and project mixing services for both commercial and musical work. Having worked both on-location and in professional studios, we pride ourselves in offering quality work within a variety of budgets, no matter the environment!

Who We Are

Nomadic Audio is compromised of audio engineers Mack Scott and Joe McNeill. Collectively, we have a decade of professional experience in the industry. We are passionate about the quality of our work and have done everything from podcasts to musical audio production!

Where We Are

Based out of Minneapolis Minnesota, Nomadic Audio is available for audio services in most of Minnesota. Because of the mobile nature of one of our sets of equipment we are able to come and help you record at the location of your choosing!
