What We Do

Nomadic Audio specializes in location-based recording and project mixing services for both commercial and musical work. Having worked both on-location and in professional studios, we pride ourselves on offering quality work within a variety of budgets, no matter the environment!

Who We Are

Nomadic Audio is compromised of audio engineers Mack Scott and Joe McNeill. Collectively, we have a decade of professional experience in the industry. We are passionate about the quality of our work and have done everything from podcasts to musical audio production!

Where We Are

Based out of Minneapolis Minnesota, Nomadic Audio is available for audio services throughout the greater Twin Cities area. Because of the mobile nature of our recording rig, we are able to come and help you record at the location of your choosing!

Meet the Crew

Joe McNeill

Joe's path in audio engineering began as two distinct childhood passions--music and technology--collating into one. From his early teens and on, the bulk of his experience has involved tracking and mixing music in location recording/project studio settings. That said, he is no stranger to commercial studio environments and other various types of projects, as well.
